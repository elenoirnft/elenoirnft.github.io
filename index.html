THE STORY
In 2446 the world is in chaos, humanity was powerless against superior entities coming from other universes. Mankind is forced to obey the will of invaders from other universes. The entity known as "Core hunter" as they're hunting for a core of a planet, committed undisputed destruction. At a time when humanity was desperate, a group of superhumans was formed thanks to the research of a scientist, they named the group "ELENOIR".


Resistance ensued and ended with the victory of ELENOIR, after the Great War ended, the side effects of the experiment starting to show, the ELENOIR began to lose consciousness one by one, the scientist sealed the Elenoir using meteorites from space to hide their inconsistency from the world so the enemy wouldn't know. To awaken them in need the power of all mankind by sending mana to the meteorite that sealed the elenoirs. Elenoir will be reborn.
